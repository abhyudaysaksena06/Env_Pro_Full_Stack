Proper Check-in & Disposal

Campus E-Waste Bins

E-waste bins have been strategically set up everywhere across the campus. Students can easily find and utilize the nearest e-waste smart bin located in their hostel lobbies, academic blocks, or cafeteria zones for convenient disposal.

Standard Disposal Process:

  • Step 1: Segregate any lithium batteries or swollen components separately.
  • Step 2: Scan the QR code on the front of the e-waste bin to log your drop-off.
  • Step 3: Deposit your unused cables, damaged circuitry, or old gadgets safely inside.
  • Step 4: Earn Eco-Points instantly towards your individual and hostel leaderboard ranking!
